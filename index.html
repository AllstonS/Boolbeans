What is Veggi-Table?

Veggi-Table is a site created to help you learn about our local CSAs and make it easier for you to figure out which one would be right for you. CSAs offer a great way for you and your family to make sure you have fresh, locally grown vegitables available at home every week. In the Charleston and outlying Charleston area, agriculture is a big part of our history and our future. We are lucky enough to have a variety of farms with wonderful familys who take great care and pride in growing high quality produce for their neighbors, which includes you! However, sometimes trying to figure out how to get started and learning what would work best for you can be difficult. We're here to make it easy. Just click on the appropriate buttons below, submit, and, voila, the CSA or CSAs best for you will appear. So go ahead, answer the questions below, and see what you find.

What Is a CSA?

CSA stands for Community Supported Agriculture. For over 25 years, CSA has become a popular way for consumers to buy local, seasonal food directly from a farmer. Here are the basics: a farmer offers a certain number of "shares" to the public. Typically the share consists of a box of vegetables, but other farm products may be included. Interested consumers purchase a share (aka a "membership" or a "subscription") and in return receive a box (bag, basket) of seasonal produce each week throughout the farming season. This arrangement creates several rewards for the consumer including eating ultra-fresh food with all the flavor and vitamin benefits, becoming exposed to new vegetables and new ways of cooking, and the convenience of simply picking up a box or bag once a week.
